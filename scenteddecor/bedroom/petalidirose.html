Petali di Rose (Rose Petals)

Intoxicating as a myriad of rose petals, the main component of this beautiful floral bouquet is precious Turkish rose, highlighted by touches of geranium, ylang ylang, violet and jasmine. Magnetic notes of cedar wood, amber, vanilla and white musk give a rich intensity to this elegant fragrance.

Petali di Rose fragrance relaxes, relieves stress and fatigue, and purifies air.
