Cuoio Nobile

NOBLE LEATHER: Exclusive and sophisticated, Cuoio Nobile is an important olfactory tribute to this precious material.

Exclusive and refined fragrance CUOIO NOBILE pays a tribute to such an important material as leather. Notes of leather wrapped in violet petals reveal in an elegant bouquet of aromas, gently combining notes of amber, white moss and vanilla. The final touches of this noble composition are precious woods: vetiver, cedar and briar.

Also available as an elegant Gift Set: Spray 100ml + Cotton Tabs

How to use: Spray is applied to the mats and fabric elements of the car. Cotton tabs are placed in the doorsâ€™ pockets, on the panel, or under the seat. From time to time, in order to strengthen the individual fragrance, spraying is recommended around the vehicle interior and on the cotton tabs to maintain the aroma. Do not spray on the indicators panel or leather upholstery, as traces of oil may remain.
